**Building the future of emergency responsiveness.**

resqme, Inc. is a safety products company dedicated to helping people prepare for the moments when seconds count. Best known for the **resqme® original car escape tool**, our mission has remained consistent for more than two decades: make compact, reliable, and accessible safety solutions that help people respond quickly in emergency situations.

Founded by Laurent Colasse after immigrating to the United States, resqme began by distributing emergency safety hammers before developing a more portable solution: a keychain-sized 2-in-1 seatbelt cutter and window breaker. The resqme® car escape tool was originally developed for first responders and has since become a globally recognized safety device for drivers, families, fleets, safety professionals, and preparedness-minded individuals. Today, more than 15 million units have been sold worldwide, and the resqme® car escape tool continues to be designed, assembled, and manufactured in the United States.  

## Our History

resqme was incorporated during the dot-com era, a time when the relationship between physical products, digital infrastructure, and public access to information was rapidly changing. While our first major innovation was physical, a compact car escape tool people could keep with them every day, our company has always understood that safety does not exist in isolation.

Emergency preparedness depends on more than a product. It depends on education, awareness, distribution, data, operational readiness, and the ability to connect the right tools with the right people at the right time.

From our earliest days, resqme has worked to make safety more accessible through practical design, strong distribution, public education, and partnerships with safety professionals. Over time, our product line has expanded to include vehicle escape tools, emergency whistles, personal alarms, roadside safety products, and preparedness solutions for consumers, businesses, first responders, and organizations.

In 2024, resqme adopted **Whistles for Life®**, a 120 dB emergency whistle created by search and rescue specialist Bob Cameron. The addition strengthened our commitment to simple, reliable safety tools that can support emergency signaling, outdoor preparedness, boating safety, search and rescue, and personal safety use cases.

## Physical Safety Meets Modern Software

As a company born during the digital transformation of the early internet, resqme continues to look for ways to connect trusted safety products with effective software stacks.

Our work today extends beyond ecommerce. We are actively exploring and building systems that improve how safety products are discovered, purchased, deployed, supported, and integrated into emergency preparedness workflows.

This includes work across:

- Modern ecommerce infrastructure
- Customer education and safety content systems
- Digital product registration and warranty workflows
- B2B, fleet, and institutional safety programs
- Emergency preparedness resources
- CRM, email, and customer communication systems
- Inventory, fulfillment, and operations software
- Data-informed marketing and safety awareness campaigns
- Future-facing emergency response and preparedness technology

Our goal is to ensure that trusted physical safety tools can synchronize with modern digital systems — from online education and product discovery to organizational deployment and future emergency response infrastructure.

## What We Build

resqme develops and distributes safety tools designed for emergency readiness, including:

- **resqme®** — The original keychain car escape tool with a seatbelt cutter and window breaker
- **resqhammer®** — A larger vehicle escape hammer with seatbelt cutter and window breaker functionality
- **Whistles for Life®** — A 120 dB emergency whistle for survival, signaling, and preparedness
- **defendme®** — Personal safety alarms for everyday carry
- **alertme®** — Driver safety and roadside preparedness tools
- Safety accessories, mounting solutions, training kits, and emergency preparedness products

Some resqme products are designed for personal everyday carry, while others are intended for vehicles, fleets, institutions, first responders, safety training, and preparedness programs.
